La Union

For many urban dwellers and Luzon residents, La Union offers some of the best beaches and surfing destinations near Manila. Because it is just a bus or car ride away from Manila, it offers a quick escape from the pressures of city living.

La Union is also known as the Garden Coast because of its Botanical Gardens found in San Fernando that showcases – a Fernery, Palmery, Fragrance Garden, Sunken Garden, Evergreen Garden, Arid Paradise, and a Shade Garden – with flora and fauna that are homegrown only in the Philippines.
